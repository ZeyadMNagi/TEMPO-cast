About Global TEMPO

Global TEMPO is an interactive platform that makes air quality and weather data easy to understand, for everyone, everywhere. For North America, we use NASA’s TEMPO satellite to deliver near-real-time air quality insights. For the rest of the world, we integrate OpenAQ and OpenWeather data to provide the closest possible view — with a clear disclaimer on data accuracy. Our goal is simple: make the invisible visible — helping communities understand their air, stay safe during pollution events, and take informed action.

Key Features

Real-Time Data

Access up-to-the-minute air quality and weather information for your location.

Global Coverage

While TEMPO focuses on North America, we provide global data through OpenAQ and OpenWeather, ensuring you stay informed no matter where you are.

Interactive Maps

Explore air quality trends with our user-friendly maps, visualizing pollution levels and weather patterns.
