About Global TEMPO

Global TEMPO is an interactive platform that makes air quality and weather data easy to understand, for everyone, everywhere. For North America, we use NASA's TEMPO satellite to deliver near-real-time air quality insights. For the rest of the world, we integrate OpenAQ and OpenWeather data to provide the closest possible view.

Disclaimer: Global data is sourced from public APIs and may not be as accurate as local government sources. Always check official advisories for health decisions.
